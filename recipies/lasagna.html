Lasagna

Description

lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

ingredients

  • beef
  • onion
  • Garlic
  • cheese
  • Eggs
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cheese Filling Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, cottage cheese works just fine in this recipe!
  2. Meat Sauce Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes.
  3. Layer Together Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
